### Job Fair [ student perspective ]

- students can upload more than CV based in the profile
- visibility based on tracks [ admin allow certain tracks or programs ]
- student can view company details
- all the students CVs is available on the site so companies can screen them and make their shortlist
- student checks in his attendance
- once he checks in he takes his position in the queue of his interviews
- students who are in interview and their turn comes , will be halted in the queue and will not lose their position
- students who are not in interview and their turn comes and didn’t attend will be moved to the end of the queue or halted in their position
- student will give a feedback on the job fair event
- company / student will fill a form if the student was accepted

### Job Fair [ admin perspective ]

- create job fair event as a draft → admin send the event invitations to companies → companies show their interesting in the event → admin confirms the job profiles   → admin publish the event → admin announce the shortlist to all the students are involved
- approve or reject companies
- approve or reject job profiles of the companies
- admin specifies the labs / halls for each company
- schedule the day of the job fair event [ agenda ]
- separate agenda for the branding day
- admin can reschedule the appointment of the companies
- can view the queues of every company - number of interviews for every company with
